About page

This web applications serves as a means to predict whether a cross-sale of insurance will be met with a positive response. The dataset contains a database of policyholders already provided with Health Insurance under this company are likely to be interested in Vehicle Insurance under the same company.

Due to the nature of this dataset inclusive language was difficult to use, thus we have to assume that the listed gender refers to the customer's legal gender/gender listed on forms. There are no gender-neutral responses, thus they were unable to be accounted for in the model.
